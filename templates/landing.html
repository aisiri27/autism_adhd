Empowering Early Autism & ADHD Screening

Early screening helps families connect to support, access therapies sooner, and improve developmental outcomes. Our prototype combines simple webcam screening and image-based analysis to provide a fast, non-invasive check.

Get Started Learn more

What is Autism?

Autism spectrum disorder (ASD) affects social communication and behavior. Early recognition can help access supports and therapies.

What is ADHD?

Attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder (ADHD) can affect attention and impulsivity. Early screening can guide behavioral support and learning plans.

How we help

Prototype screening uses non-invasive visual cues and emotion/attention metrics to provide a risk score — always presented as a screening result, not a diagnosis.

Why Early Screening Matters

Early identification of developmental differences gives children access to targeted interventions during critical growth windows. Families can better understand needs, teachers can adapt learning environments, and clinicians can plan appropriate assessments.
